# App Store description — Talkie for iPhone

Finalized 2026-07-16.

---

Talkie is a voice memo app with live transcription and a dictation keyboard.

Record a thought in one tap and watch the transcript take shape while you speak. Find any memo later by what you said. When something's worth keeping, clean it up with AI and send it to your Mac to become real work.

CATCH THE THOUGHT
• Start a voice memo in one tap
• Follow the live transcript as you speak
• Find past memos by what you said

DICTATE ANYWHERE
• Dictate into Messages, Notes, Mail, and more with the Talkie keyboard
• Keep composing without jumping between apps
• Turn rough speech into clear writing

STAY IN CONTROL OF AI EDITS
• Ask for a rewrite in your own words
• Compare the before and after
• Accept only the changes you want

CHOOSE HOW TALKIE WORKS
• Use Apple Speech or a local Parakeet model for transcription
• Connect your own AI provider or route work through your Mac
• Keep recordings and transcripts in sync with iCloud

No Talkie account is required. Your captures live on your devices and in your iCloud account.
