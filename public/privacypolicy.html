Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 5, 2025

Overview

Talkie is designed with your privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not collect, access, or store any of your personal information.

Data Storage

All your voice memos, transcriptions, and app data are stored locally on your device and synced through your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. This data is only accessible to you through your Apple ID.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. We do not have access to your recordings, transcriptions, or any other content you create in the app.

Third-Party Services

Talkie uses Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) for syncing your data across your devices. This service is governed by Apple's privacy policy. We do not use any other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Audio Processing

Voice recordings and transcriptions are processed on your device. Your audio data is never sent to our servers because we don't have any servers that receive your data.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@usetalkie.com
